Korean Food Map

Welcome to Korean Food Map in Atlanta

As a native speaker, I was surprised to learn that Atlanta has the sixth largest Korean community in America, and seeing so many Korean signs in Buford Highway supported the claim. However, when I started looking around to find good Korean restaurants, I have had more failures than success. Then I realized the information I used to get from Yelp or Foursquare was not as great as suggestions from other native speakers. That's when I started collecting my own lists to create this website.

By clicking the pins on the map, you will see not just curated Korean restaurant information, but description about the iconic food that I love and used to ear when I lived in South Korea. I understand it's hard to try a complete-strange-dish, so I explained ingredients, reasons I love this particular dish, and how spicy it is.

To explore Korean restaurants and learn more about different Korean dishes, start clicking pins!
